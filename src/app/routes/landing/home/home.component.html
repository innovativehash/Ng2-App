We focus on key questions
within the 6 pillars of our IT due dilligence framework

IT Strategy

  • Alignment of the IT strategy with the business strategy

IT Spending

  • Reasonableness of IT spending levels compared to benchmarks
  • Run-rate(Opex) vs. One-time Costs (Capex)

Business Applications

  • Stability and scalability of the application portfolio
  • Use of commercial packages vs. Custom applications
  • Integrated vs. standalone applications
  • Level of process automation

IT Organization

  • Leadership and appropriate skillsets
  • In house resources vs. effective use of third party support

IT Projects

  • Captial, timing and resources requirement for current and future projects
  • Potential risks of failure(delays, additional costs)

Infrastructure & Operation

  • Scalability and redundancy of the IT infrastructure
  • Preparedness for disaster recovery, change management, business continuity, and service management
